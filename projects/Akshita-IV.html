Akshita Golden Breez - IV

Quick Summary

  • Location: Maheswaram, Hyderabad
  • Landmark: Pulimamidi Road Near Electronic City SEZ & Wipro Company
  • Project Type: Open Plots
  • Approval: HMDA & RERA
  • RERA NO: P02400005008
  • Project Size: 24 Acres
  • Bank Loan: Available

Project Description

Akshita Golden Breez - IV presents prime open plots in Maheswaram, Hyderabad, near Electronic City SEZ. With HMDA and RERA approvals, spread over 24 acres, these plots offer essential amenities like avenue plantation, BT roads, and underground utilities. Strategically located, it provides easy access to Electronic City, HCL, TATA, Wipro SEZ, ORR, RRR, and the airport. Ideal for investment in Hyderabad's booming real estate market.
